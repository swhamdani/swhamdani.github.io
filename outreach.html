Science Communication & Community

Public Outreach & Volunteer Work

Communicating cybersecurity research and policy to general audiences is not a side activity — it is a responsibility. This page documents sustained public engagement since 2022 alongside community and humanitarian service.

Platform

CyberWalk.pk — Cybersecurity Awareness

Founded in 2023, CyberWalk.pk is a Pakistani cybersecurity awareness platform aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). It produces regular articles, analyses, and public education content in English and Urdu, reaching school teachers, parents, students, and professionals across Pakistan.

Recognition

CyberWalk.pk was the basis for selection as an ISOC Community Fellow 2025 by the Internet Society, recognizing the platform's contributions to cybersecurity awareness and digital community empowerment.

Community

Cybersecurity Awareness Sessions

Since 2022, I have conducted cybersecurity awareness workshops and training sessions for school teachers, parents, and students. The sessions aim to extend practical cybersecurity knowledge to everyday digital citizens — with a goal of expanding this initiative nationwide and to diverse professionals including lawyers, healthcare workers, and educators.

Peer reviewer for the Journal of Network and Computer Applications (Elsevier) since 2020, contributing to research quality in network security.

Volunteer

Humanitarian & Volunteer Service

Oct 2005
AJK, Pakistan

Earthquake Relief — Azad Jammu & Kashmir

Volunteered for two months in AJK following the catastrophic earthquake of October 8, 2005, assisting with the management and distribution of relief supplies for survivors. As a universal blood donor, donated blood multiple times during this period and remained available on call for ongoing humanitarian needs. This experience continues to inform a deep commitment to community service.
