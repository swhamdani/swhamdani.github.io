Outreach and Volunteer Work

Science communication, cybersecurity awareness, and community service since 2022.

CyberWalk.pk

Founded in 2023, CyberWalk.pk is a cybersecurity awareness platform aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals. It produces regular articles, analyses, and public education content in English and Urdu, reaching school teachers, parents, students, and professionals across Pakistan. The platform was recognized by the Internet Society as the basis for the ISOC Community Fellowship 2025 award.

Since 2022, I have also conducted in-person cybersecurity awareness workshops for school teachers, parents, and students, with the goal of extending this initiative nationwide and to diverse professional groups including lawyers and healthcare workers.
